About the Game

Hadel Water is an open-world fishing adventure where you can explore vast and uncharted seas teeming with countless species of fish, from tiny schools that shimmer just beneath the surface to rare and exotic catches hidden in the depths.

Face massive beasts rising from the abyss to challenge your survival, gather wealth, uncover secrets, and upgrade your boat and gear to conquer both the waters and the monsters that dwell within them.
